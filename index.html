Unit 25: Expressing Purpose (表達目的)

1. 為了... (To / In order to / So as to)

  • to + verb (最常見): She came to help us.
  • in order to / so as to (較正式): He took a taxi in order to get there on time.
  • ⚠️ 否定用法注意：必須是 in order not to 或 so as not to。(不可以只寫 not to)
  • in order that / so (that) + 子句: I exercise so that I can keep fit. (後方常接 can/will/could)

2. 事物的用途 (For + Noun / V-ing)

  • 表達物品功能：We use this key to open / for opening the cupboard. (兩者皆可)
  • ⚠️ 表達「人」的目的時，不可用 for + V-ing。
    ✅ She went to a restaurant for lunch.
    ❌ She went to a restaurant for having lunch.

3. 以防萬一 / 避免 (In case / Prevent)

  • in case (以防): Take an umbrella in case it rains. (⚠️ 後面不用 will，用現在式代未來)
  • 表達目的的動詞：enable (使能夠), avoid (避免 + V-ing), prevent (阻止).

Unit 24: Cause & Effect (因果關係)

1. 名詞與動詞用法 (Nouns & Verbs)

  • 名詞：the reason for / the cause of / the result of
  • 動詞：
    A resulted in / led to / caused B (A 導致 B)
    B resulted from A (B 起因於 A)

2. 因為 / 由於 (Prepositions & Conjunctions)

接名詞 (Prepositions) 接子句 (Conjunctions)
due to / owing to / because of / on account of + Noun
例: The delay was due to the strike. 		because / since / as / for + 子句
例: I went to bed early because I was tired.
⚠️ For 不可放在句首。
out of (出於情感) 例: out of pity
through (由於) 例: through carelessness 		Now (that) / Seeing that (既然)
例: Seeing that it's raining, we'd better cancel.

3. 太...而不能 / 足夠... (Too / Enough)

  • He has too much pride to accept help. (他太驕傲而無法接受幫助)
  • This book is not easy enough for me to read.
  • ⚠️ 注意：句尾不要加上多餘的代名詞 (❌ The shirt is too large for me to wear it.)

Unit 23: Adverbials (副詞與狀語)

1. 表達方式 (Manner)

  • 副詞 (Adverb)：通常字尾加 -ly (fluent ➔ fluently)。
  • 片語 (Phrases)：
    in a ... way / manner (例: in his own way)
    with / without + Noun (例: with sympathy, without delay)

2. Like vs. As vs. As if

字詞 用法與例句
like (像) 介係詞，後接名詞/代名詞/V-ing。
例: She cried like a child.
as (如/作為) 1. 連接詞，接子句：I put it back as you told me to.
2. 介係詞，表職業身分：He worked as a teacher.
as if / as though (彷彿) 若與事實不符，需使用假設語氣 (過去式)。
例: You are talking to me as if you were my mother.
若動作發生在更早之前，用過去完成式 (as if he had witnessed it).

3. 手段與工具 (Means & Instruments)

  • with (用...工具): wrap the gift with special paper.
  • by (藉由...方法/交通): by bus, by train, by breaking the lock. (⚠️ 走路是 on foot)
  • through (透過): climb through a window.

4. 必背固定搭配 (Collocations)

常考的介係詞搭配，請務必熟記：

by mistake (不小心)
by chance (偶然)
by hand (手工)
in a hurry (匆忙)
in pencil (用鉛筆)
on purpose (故意)
on foot (走路)
with care (小心地)
